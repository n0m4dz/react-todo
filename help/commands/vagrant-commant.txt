vagrant init -> ankh vagrant-g achaallah
vagrant up -> virtual mashiniig asaah (herev ankh asaaj bgaa bol provision unshina)
vagrant reload -> restart hiih
vagrant halt -> shutdown hiih
vagrant [reload, up] --provision -> provision tohirgoog unshij asaah
vagrant destroy -> tuhain box-g bur moson ustgah
vagrant share -> tunnel uusgej online bolgoh
vagrant box list -> suusan box-uudiin jagsaaltiig harah
